For our third year project, we have created One Pass, a system inspired by event-hosting platforms, for joining and hosting events for students. The event participants could easily search for events, join them quickly with pre-filled form, and attend the event with a handy QR code to register. Meanwhile, the event organizer would have a hub to advertise their events, provide their registration form conveniently, and effortlessly manage event participants!
