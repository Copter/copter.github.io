Hatchling

 -

 https://github.com/Copter/unity-hatchling

 2018

 Personal Project

 Scripting in Unity Game Engine, Art & Animation

Hatchling is a pet simulator mobile game. Inspired by Tamagotchi, the game features the three main buttons. Players can interact with their virtual pet, earn coins, and buy cosmetics! There is also a mini-game that rewards even more coins!
