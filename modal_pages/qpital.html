Qpital

 -

 2021

 User Interface Design

 -

 Arin Thirasawasd, Intuorn Jariyaprasertsin, Janettre Kaivipakbanyai

For our User Interface Design course, we have to come up with an idea for an application that can aid people during a pandemic. Qpital would be an application that patients can easily find the nearest hospital or clinic, check-in easily through QR code, and receive a queue number in the app. This would eliminate the problem of searching for hospitals, difficulties of checking-in, and the need to sit in a waiting room!
