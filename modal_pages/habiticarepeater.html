Habitica is a "gamified" productivity website/app that allow users to list their habits and tasks which can be scored when they are completed. Users can gain in-game currency, experience points, and items when they score a task or a positive habit.

Habitica Repeater allow users to check off their "habits" multiple times at once. For example, if I listed a task "Study for 20 minutes," but I study for 2 hours, after my studying session, I can score 6 times just by typing a number and click "+", instead of clicking "+" 6 times on the Habitica website.
