 Game Research Club 2018

 Scripting in Unity Game Engine, Pixel Art & Animation

 Kiattipoom Rojvanakarn, Nathon Techasrisutee

Project GRC 2018 was made as a part of the annual Chulalongkorn University's Game Research Club showcase. Our group has created a top-down action RPG prototype. In this game, the player must find openings in the enemy's attack and strike accrodingly. The player can also block or dodge roll to get out of harm's way.
