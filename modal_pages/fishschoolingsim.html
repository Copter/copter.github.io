 2021-2022

 Senior Project

 Unity Scripting

 Norapat Buppodom, Ravipas Wangananont

Simulation of large homogeneous fish schools in the presence of food and predators

This project aims to simulate schooling behavior of fish in Unity 2D Environment by using Machine Learning, which controls through Unity's ML-Agents. During the project's development, the Unity team has created a simulated environment which includes fish, food zone, walls, and predator. Meanwhile, the Machine Learning team tested the simulation through three algorithms: NEAT, Genetic Algorithm, and Deep Reinforcement Learning.
