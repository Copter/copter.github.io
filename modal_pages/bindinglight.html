Binding Light

 -

 https://github.com/Copter/unity-bindingLight

 2018

 Personal Project

 Scripting in Unity Game Engine, Art & Animation

Binding Light is an endless, arcade-inspired game for touchscreen phones. Using two fingers, the player must guide two circles through horde of green squares. The line between the two circles can remove the squares and score points. Players can also aim for combos by removing multiple squares quickly and gain extra points! The game ends when any circle collides with a green square.
