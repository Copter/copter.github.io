 Play the demo on itch.io!

 2020 - 2022

 Personal Project

 Scripting in Godot Game Engine, Art, Animation & Music

Dino-sword is a hop 'n' slash Action RPG inspired by Adventure Time's Bakery & Bravery with a Souls-like progression system. The player must try to get close and attack enemies, meanwhile ducking under high attacks and dodging large swings or area-of-effect attacks. The player can also use abilities and upgrades to ensure their victory.
