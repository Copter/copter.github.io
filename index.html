Dino-sword
Dino-sword is a 2D hack'n'slash platformer created in Godot engine. The game mechanics are inspired by Dark Souls (Stamina management, Action commitment) and Infinity Blade (Directional parry.) The player can attack in 4 directions: up swing, down swing, forward swing, and backward swing. By observing the enemies' attack, the player can respond with the correct swing to parry the incoming blow.
